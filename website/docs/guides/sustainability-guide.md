# Sustainability Guide

Nova Ecosystem embeds sustainability into every layer.

## Environmental
- Support Digital Product Passports (DPP).
- Automate Life Cycle Assessment (LCA).

## Social
- Ensure fair wage auditing via NovaEquity.
- Inclusive onboarding for displaced experts.

## Economic
- Enable Product‑as‑a‑Service models.
- Circular marketplaces for reuse and refurbishment.
