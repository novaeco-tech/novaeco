# Level 3: Medium (Value Chain Coordination)

**Focus:** B2B, compliance, audits, and optimization.

---

## <a id="M1"></a> [M1] Startup Funding

**Description**  
Bioplastics startup matches with Investor, Lab, and Mentor.

**Challenges**  
- Due diligence  
- IP protection  

**Sustainability Problem**  
Accelerates circular innovation capital deployment.

**User Interaction**  
Startup posts "Needs" on NovaHub → AI matches with Angel Investor (Capital) and NovaInfra (Lab) → Deal signed via NovaFin.

**Ecosystem Usage**  
NovaHub, NovaFin, NovaInfra

---

## <a id="M2"></a> [M2] Furniture Loop

**Description**  
Siemens sells 500 used chairs to Munich Re.

**Challenges**  
- Bulk logistics  
- Condition assessment  

**Sustainability Problem**  
Extends product life; reduces furniture waste.

**User Interaction**  
Siemens lists assets on NovaTrade → Munich Re buys → NovaMobility auto‑books truck → NovaBuild updates building inventory.

**Ecosystem Usage**  
NovaTrade, NovaMobility, NovaBuild

---

## <a id="M3"></a> [M3] Compliance Check

**Description**  
Textile importer validates supplier certs against EU laws.

**Challenges**  
- Fraudulent documents  
- Changing regulations  

**Sustainability Problem**  
Enforces ethical/environmental standards in supply chain.

**User Interaction**  
Importer uploads PDF certs to NovaPolicy → NovaSapien extracts data → Checks against EU database → Validates.

**Ecosystem Usage**  
NovaPolicy, NovaSapien, NovaTextile

---

## <a id="M4"></a> [M4] Smart Bin Route

**Description**  
Waste route optimized based on bin fullness sensors.

**Challenges**  
- Sensor reliability  
- Dynamic routing for drivers  

**Sustainability Problem**  
Reduces collection truck CO₂; prevents bin overflow.

**User Interaction**  
Bins send level data to NovaWaste → `novamobility-worker-logistics-optimizer` calculates route → Pushed to driver app.

**Ecosystem Usage**  
NovaWaste, NovaMobility, NovaSapien

---

## <a id="M10"></a> [M10] Solvent Lease

**Description**  
Auto plant pays for "cleaning service" (Chemical Leasing).

**Challenges**  
- Business model shift (volume to service)  
- On‑site recycling  

**Sustainability Problem**  
Reduces chemical consumption; incentivizes recovery.

**User Interaction**

**Ecosystem Usage**
